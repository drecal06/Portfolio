Photography

Background

Since I could remember I loved taking photos, I would bring my 3ds everywhere since it was the only thing that had a camera I owned. In 2017 I bought my first canon camera and started to take photography seriously. I eventually upgraded my litle point in shoot into a canon M50 mkii. In the fall of 2021 I began to work as the Pasadena Baseball Social Media manager.

In the fall of 2023 I joined yearbook as a photographer and learned even more. I am still the Social media Manager for the Pasadena Baseball team. Some clients I have worked for are, Pasadena ASB, Pasadena High School, and CBA Baseball.
